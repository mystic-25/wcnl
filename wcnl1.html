Formula 1 (F1)

Definition: Formula 1 (F1) is the highest class of international single-seater auto racing sanctioned by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA). It blends state-of-the-art engineering with incredible driving skill.

F1 racing features teams and drivers competing in Grands Prix worldwide. Cars use hybrid power units and advanced aerodynamics — every millimeter and watt matters on track.
